2.1.16 problem 18

P(A|B)=P(A∩B)P(B)=P(B)−P(B∩Ac)P(B)=1−P(B∩Ac)P(B).

Note that P(Ac)=P(Ac∩B)+P(Ac∩Bc). Hence, P(B∩Ac)=P(Ac)−P(Ac∩Bc)=0−P(Ac∩Bc)=−P(Ac∩Bc).

Since probabilities are nonnegative, this implies P(B∩Ac)=P(Ac∩Bc)=0.

Thus, P(A|B)=1.
